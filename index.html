The Designer

Karina Kanha YIN

"Couture is not clothing — it is confidence given form, dreams made tangible."

Trained in Paris and refined through years working alongside the great houses, Karina Fontaine founded her private atelier with a singular conviction: every woman deserves a garment that was made for her alone.

Drawing from a philosophy of feminine strength and quiet elegance, each piece is a collaboration between designer and client — a shared journey of discovery that results in something truly irreplaceable.
