The Bridal Collection

A Gown Born From Your Story

At Maison Elara, a bridal gown is never taken from a rack. It begins with a conversation — about who you are, who you are becoming, and how you wish to feel as you walk toward the person you love.

From that first meeting, every decision — silhouette, fabric, embellishment, train length — is made entirely for you. The result is a gown that could not exist for anyone else.

Fabrics

Veils & Accessories

"She was wearing the most beautiful thing I had ever seen — and it was made for her alone."

Available exclusively as a bespoke commission. Minimum 6 months lead time required.
