The Collection

Draped in Darkness & Light

The Evening Couture collection is an ode to the hours between dusk and dawn — when a woman steps into a room and the world pauses. Each gown is constructed with architectural precision and finished with the hand-sewn detail that only true couture can offer.

From cascading silk charmeuse to sculptural duchess satin, every silhouette is designed around the woman who will wear it: her posture, her movement, her story.

Silhouettes

Column, A-line, mermaid, and sculptural ballgown forms — each tailored to your individual proportions and movement.

Fabrics

Silk charmeuse, duchess satin, hand-beaded organza, velvet, and Chantilly lace sourced from Maison Lesage, Lyon.

Embellishment

Hand-sewn crystal embroidery, feather trim, custom pleating, and couture-level beadwork — applied stitch by stitch.

Timeline

From first consultation to delivery: 6–8 weeks. Rush commissions available upon request with atelier availability.

Featured Piece

La Nuit Étoilée

"She walked into the room and the evening began."

A floor-length column gown in ivory silk charmeuse, hand-embroidered with over 2,400 individual crystals arranged in the pattern of the winter constellation. The back falls open in a deep V, secured by a single silk bow.

Exclusively available as a bespoke commission. Each piece is created once — for one woman, for one evening.
